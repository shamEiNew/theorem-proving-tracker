Formal Methods + Lean + ML/AI Roadmap

42-week plan: build strong foundations in formal methods & mathematics while continuously shipping small, visible projects (Lean repos, ML notebooks, AI-for-Lean tools).
Stages: S1 Lean Basics · S2 Math + Lean · S3 Formal Methods · S4 ML Foundations · S5 AI-for-Lean · S6 Integration & Portfolio.
Hours/week: suggested 7–10 hours Deliverable: something you can show on GitHub/blog every few weeks Remarks: blockers, ideas, or links
